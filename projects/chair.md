Chairs are vital to human. They supports our backs, give us a better posture and even help to relieve our stress. But the most important thing is that they're everywhere, with variety of shapes and colors; so finding a chair might be challenging. With the help of Computer Vision, this report focus on pointing out chairs in images. Multiple chairs detection and semantic segmentation models are investigated on ADE20K Dataset.
